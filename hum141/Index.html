United States Wealth Inequality
By Sharif Bey

In our current society there is more wealth inequality than ever before and it has gotten significantly worse in the last 100 years. As of 2022 three multibillionares have more overall wealth than the entire bottom of 160 million Americans 45 percent of new income goes to the top 1% of people and CEOs make 350 times more than their average workers. This is a problem that needs to be addressed but how?

We need to redefine what we consider wealth inequality if we ever wish to have an equal distribution of wealth, huge gaps like this between rich and poor are oppressive and lack ethics.
