If you still believe Michael Jordan is better than Lebron James as an Individual (now that we have distinguished the difference between the greatness of an individual and the greatness of a team) then what if I told you that almost every stat would prove you wrong? Lebron scores almost as many points a game while rebounding, passing, and shooting 3-pointers much better. While the 2 may be close in scoring defense and personal accolades Lebron is levels ahead of in other aspects of the game. Specifically clutch shooting, rebounding, and passing. This is the reason his impact on the game is so much greater than Jordan's and why he is able to win with any teammates. This part is also due to the fact that in some ways he is a bigger stronger Michael Jordan with a better basketball IQ. Lebron is listed at almost 6 10 260 and Jordan was listed at 6 6 220. Lebron can run faster is stronger and can jump just as high as Michael Jordan with a similar (and in some ways better) skillset. So you tell me who would you pick: a bigger player who can do more or a smaller player who is arguably only slightly better at one thing but is nowhere near as advanced in other areas.
